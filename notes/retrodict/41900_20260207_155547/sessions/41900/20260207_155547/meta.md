Question: Will OpenAI release GPT-6 for general public availability before May 1, 2026?
Final forecast: ~10% probability
Approach: Researched OpenAI's release timeline, Altman's statements, Manifold market prices, and applied Nothing Ever Happens reasoning.

- No Polymarket markets found for this specific question

# Research Notes: BIS Semiconductor Export Control Final Rules

## Question
Will the US BIS publish a final rule tightening semiconductor-related export controls before May 1, 2026?

## Key Findings

### Qualifying Final Rules (published before May 1, 2026)

1. **December 5, 2024 - Entity List Final Rule** ✅ FINAL RULE, TIGHTENS
   - Title: "Additions and Modifications to the Entity List; Removals From the Validated End-User (VEU) Program"
   - Federal Register: 2024-28267
   - Action: Final rule
   - Summary explicitly states: "All of these entities...are involved with the development and production of 'advanced-node integrated circuits' ('advanced-node ICs') and/or semiconductor manufacturing items"
   - Adds 140 entities to Entity List, modifies 14, removes 3 from VEU
   - BIS press release: "Commerce Strengthens Export Controls to Restrict China's Capability to Produce Advanced Semiconductors"

2. **September 2, 2025 - VEU Revocation Final Rule** ✅ FINAL RULE, TIGHTENS
   - Title: "Revocation of Validated End-User Authorizations in the People's Republic of China"
   - Action: Final rule
   - Removes Intel Semiconductor (Dalian), Samsung China Semiconductor, SK hynix Semiconductor from VEU program
   - Requires individual export licenses instead of VEU authorization
   - Effective December 31, 2025

### Non-qualifying rules

3. **January 15, 2026 - License Review Policy** ❌ LOOSENS controls
   - Title: "Revision to License Review Policy for Advanced Computing Commodities"
   - Action: Final rule
   - Moves from presumption of denial to case-by-case review for certain chips to China
   - This LOOSENS, not tightens

4. **January 15, 2025 - AI Diffusion Framework** ❌ INTERIM final rule (not final)
   - Rescinded by Trump administration May 2025

5. **December 5, 2024 - FDP Rule Additions** ❌ INTERIM final rule (not final)
   - Major semiconductor control expansion but wrong rule type

### Political Context (Trump Administration)
- May 2025: Rescinded Biden AI Diffusion Rule
- January 2026: Loosened chip export licensing to China
- Overall direction: loosening controls, favoring tariffs over export controls
- BIS said it would issue replacement rule but hasn't yet

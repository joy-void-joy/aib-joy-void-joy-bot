# Copyright Office AI Report Part 4 - Research Notes

## Question
Will the U.S. Copyright Office publish a report document labeled Part 4 in its Copyright and Artificial Intelligence series before May 1, 2026?

## Key Findings

### Series Structure

- Part 2: Copyrightability - Published January 29, 2025
- Part 3: Generative AI Training - Pre-publication version released May 9, 2025; FINAL VERSION NOT YET PUBLISHED

- McDermott Will: "the third and **likely final** installment"
- Neal & Leroy (Jan 2026): "the **final two installments** of its comprehensive study"
- Part 2 announcement: "The report is being released in **three** Parts"

- Courts reinstated her (Sept 2025 appeals court ruling)

- Part 1: Digital replicas

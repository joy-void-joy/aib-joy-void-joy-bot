# Research Notes: Google TPU at Cloud Next 2026 (Q43121)

## Question
Will Google announce a new named TPU generation (beyond Ironwood) at Cloud Next 2026 (April 22-24)?

## Key Findings

### TPU v8 Development Status
- **Confirmed in development** by multiple industry sources:
  - SemiAnalysis: "TPUv8AX" (codename Sunfish) and "TPUv8X" (codename Zebrafish)
  - JY Industry Insights: "TPU Generation 8, codenamed Zebra/Sun Fish, projected to launch H2 2026"
  - Paradis Labs (X/Twitter analyst): "$AVGO are co-architects for TPU v8 and v9"
- **NOT officially announced by Google** as of April 10, 2026
- Google Cloud docs still list TPU7x (Ironwood) as latest

### Broadcom Deal (April 6, 2026)
- Broadcom signed long-term agreement to supply "future generations of TPUs" through 2031
- Anthropic gets ~3.5 GW of "next-generation TPU capacity" starting 2027
- Deal covers networking + components for "next-generation AI racks"
- No specific TPU generation named in the filing

### Historical TPU Announcement Pattern
- TPU v1: Google I/O 2016
- TPU v2: Google I/O 2017
- TPU v3: Google I/O 2018
- TPU v4: Google I/O 2021
- TPU v5e: Cloud Next 2023 (Aug)
- TPU v5p: Dec 2023 (separate)
- Trillium/v6e: Google I/O 2024 (May)
- **Ironwood/v7: Cloud Next 2025 (April) → GA Nov 2025**

### Cloud Next New TPU History
- 2023: YES (v5e)
- 2024: NO (focused on v5p GA + Axion)
- 2025: YES (Ironwood/v7)
- Base rate: 2/3 but tiny sample

### Preview/Preview Articles
- GW Apps "What to Expect" only mentions "Updates on 7th Gen TPUs" - no new generation
- No leaked agenda items about new TPU gen

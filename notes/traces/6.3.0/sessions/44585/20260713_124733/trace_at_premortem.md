## Overview

The **National Preparedness Level (PL)** is a 1–5 scale set by the **National Multi-Agency Coordinating Group (NMAC/NMAC)** at the National Interagency Fire Center (NIFC) in Boise, Idaho. **PL5** is the highest level, indicating that "the nation's wildland firefighting resources are committed to the limit" — geographic areas are heavily engaged and additional national/international measures (e.g., military, international resources) may be mobilized.

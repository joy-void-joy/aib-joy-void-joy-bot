**Neither Anthropic nor Mistral AI is listed as a signatory of the European Commission's EU AI Pact voluntary pledges** — as of the current official signatories list (checked July 2026) and across the entire 2024–2026 period.

## Background on the EU AI Pact

- The **EU AI Pact** is a voluntary initiative launched by the European Commission to encourage organizations to proactively begin applying the principles of the AI Act ahead of its full legal application. The AI Act itself entered into force on **August 1, 2024**.
- The Pact is distinct from the legally-oriented **General-Purpose AI (GPAI) Code of Practice** (a separate voluntary compliance tool published in July 2025). This distinction matters because Anthropic and Mistral have different statuses on the two instruments.
- First signatories were announced by the Commission on **September 25, 2024**, with **over 100 companies** in the first wave.
- The list has grown over time to **more than 230 companies** (per the official page as of the current period), and the Commission notes it is "continuously updated."

## Findings on Anthropic and Mistral AI (AI Pact)

**The current official signatories list at `digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/ai-pact` does NOT contain either "Anthropic" or "Mistral AI."** I reviewed the full alphabetical list (~230+ entries). Notably, some AI/LLM firms ARE present — e.g., **OpenAI, Microsoft, Amazon, Google-adjacent partners, Cohere, Aleph Alpha, IBM, SAP, Palantir** — but Anthropic and Mistral are absent.

- **Anthropic**: Confirmed as a non-signatory. At the September 2024 launch, Anthropic was described as a "notable absence" from the AI Pact given its prominence in AI development. It remains absent from the current list.

## Important Distinction — the GPAI Code of Practice (a DIFFERENT instrument)

This is a key nuance to avoid conflation:
- **Anthropic DID sign the EU's General-Purpose AI Code of Practice** (announced July 2025) — but this is a separate voluntary tool from the AI Pact.

## Executive Summary

This question asks whether the Trump administration will initiate *another* formal process to reduce NYC funding, beyond the October 2025 transit funding withholding (~$18B). After researching, I found multiple formal actions have already occurred since October 2025, including the HHS funding freeze (January 2026) affecting New York state and the explicit listing of "New York City, NY" on the DOJ sanctuary jurisdiction list under EO 14287.

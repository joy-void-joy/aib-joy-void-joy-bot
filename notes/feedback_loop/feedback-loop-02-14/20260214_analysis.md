## Object-Level Findings

### Subagent Usage Analysis (Primary Finding)

**0 out of 12 live v1.0.0 forecasts used subagents.** When subagents were used in 4 retrodict traces, they produced duplicate work with tiny outputs. The agent's natural workflow (data gathering -> Monte Carlo simulation -> meta-reflection -> output) doesn't benefit from parallelized subagents.

**Decision: Remove subagent infrastructure entirely.**
- Deleted `spawn_subagents` tool and all subagent types (researcher, analyst, premortem)
- Kept `spawn_subquestions` for compound question decomposition
- Removed `src/aib/agent/subagents.py` (177 lines)
- Removed 15 dead Pydantic output models from `models.py` (~216 lines)
- Simplified `composition.py` from 524 to 197 lines
- Removed ~25 lines of subagent guidance from system prompt
- Net: 906 lines deleted

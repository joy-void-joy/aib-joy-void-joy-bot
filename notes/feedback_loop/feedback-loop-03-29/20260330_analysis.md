## v3.5.0 Stock Pattern: Systematic Regime Blindness (NEW FINDING)

Examined 13 v3.5.0 stock forecasts (42387, 42390, 42393, 42402, 42404, 42409, 42411, 42413, 42417, 42419, 42421, 42423, 42428). All 13 resolved NO during a sustained market decline (early March 2026).

**Pattern:**
1. Agent computes historical conditional base rate from `stock_conditional_returns` → ~52-54%
2. S&P 500 decline cited as "slight headwind" → subtract 1-2pp
3. End up at 50-55% (slightly bullish)
4. Stock goes down → Brier 0.25-0.30 on every one

**Root cause:** `stock_conditional_returns` pools events from all market regimes (1927-present). During a sustained multi-week decline, the true probability of "stock up over 7 days" is substantially below 50%, but the tool returns the long-run average that includes recovery rallies.

**Contrast with v3.6.0:** 3/4 stocks resolved YES two weeks later (market recovery). Same methodology, different regime — agent happened to be closer to correct.

**Gap:** The tool conditions on the individual stock's drawdown but not on the market regime (VIX, SPX trend, recession indicator). The agent at post 42693 explicitly noted: "No tool to condition returns on BOTH drawdown level AND market volatility regime."
